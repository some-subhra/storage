FAQs
What can you help me with?

Wazzeer is the 'go-to-destination' for all your legal, account and compliance needs. Through our set of handpicked partners (Lawyers, Chartered Accountants and Company Secretaries) we offer end-to-end solutions (barring litigation and criminal law matters, as of now) - practically everything a lawyer, CA or CS can offer.

You can find the complete list of services we offer on the following link: Link of complete list of services

How do I avail the services? What is the process?

Visit www.wazzeer.com and select the area in which you need support from the listed services either from the home page or from the services page from the menu. Select a sub-service area and share the basic information that we need. In case you are not sure of the answers or service area, there is nothing to worry about. Answer the questions to the best of your knowledge and understanding. We will give you a call and you can share the details at that time. Once we have the details, we will share quotations with you along with the profile of the professionals. You can select your preferred quotation and we can go ahead.

Alternatively, you can simply leave your contact details and request a call back. We will reach out to you and can take up from there.

Once I share my requirements, how does it work?

The Wazzeer team will get in touch with you and try to better understand your requirements and clarify any doubt that we might have. Post this call, we will share quotations and profiles with you. You can select the service provider of your choice. Once the service provider is selected, you will need to deposit the fees with us – treat this similar to blocking of the amount. As and when the work is completed and a milestone is reached, the agreed amount (as agreed in the quotation) would be released to the service provider, post your consent. At any point if you are unsatisfied with the service, you can stop the release of the payment.

The service provider works and shares regular updates and work-in-progress versions with you. The Wazzeer team would be a part of the loop and constantly in touch with you and manage the project on your behalf. At any point, if you are not happy with the way things are progressing, you can raise a flag and the Wazzeer team will intervene. Once the work is completed, you can review and let us know if it is of the quality you expected or not. In case you are not happy with the work quality, you can request for the changes/ rework within 5 working days.

How does the payment work?

Once you have selected the service provider and the quotation, you will need to deposit the fees with us – treat this similar to blocking of the amount. As and when the work is completed and a milestone is reached, the agreed amount (as agreed in the quotation) would be released to the service provider, post your consent. At any point if you are unsatisfied with the service, you can stop the release of the payment. To deposit the payment with us, you can do an online transfer to our account. We will share the details along with the quotation.

My requirements are not fully covered in your service list. Does that mean you cant help me?

Do reach out to us. We will understand your requirements and customize our service to meet them, if possible.

Do you offer services in my city?

Many of the services we offer are not location dependent. We have set up mechanism and leverage technology to deliver quality service online. For these type of work, we can offer our services irrespective of your city.

There are other services which require only a minor level of documentation locally - for example, notarization for a Private Limited Company registration. In case you can get this notarization done locally, with our team supporting you over phone and mails, we can complete the work without any hiccups.

However, there are certain types of work that require an extensive on ground work - for example, VAT registration. We offer these services only in the cities where we have an on-ground presence.

Right now we have an on-ground presence in the following cities: Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Mumbai and Pune. We are growing and working towards covering other cities. In case you need help in a city apart from the ones listed here, please do reach out to us. We might be able to help you using our network.

What kind of professional would be working on my requirements?

We do realize the importance of the services we offer and how critical the quality of delivery can be. To ensure quality, we have developed a network of closely associated partners or service providers. These are qualified lawyers, CA and CS who work on the delivery of the work that comes to us.

We have developed a robust method of selecting the Partners. We are not looking at getting everyone onboard. Instead, the focus is on quality. We are getting partners whom we have worked with in the past and we know of their quality of work and professionalism. In case we do not know them from before, we are getting them onboard only through strong referrals. In short, we are getting partners we can vouch for in terms of capabilities, quality and professionalism.

What if the job takes longer than the estimated time?

We ensure that a realistic timeline is promised and manage the work efficiently. However, the nature of legal and accounting work and the dependencies they have is such that there is a chance of work overshooting the expected timeline. While we cannot guarantee that there wont be any delay, we can assure is that you will be kept in the loop and updated with all the information we have. In case we see any possible delay, you will be intimated at the earliest so that you can plan accordingly.

How do you ensure quality of work?

We believe that the most important step for ensuring quality of work is getting the right partners (or service providers). We spend considerable time and efforts in making the right choice.

Standard operating practices and use of the latest project management tools ensure a smooth process and regular updates to all the stakeholders. We will have someone from the Wazzeer team as your point of contact who will be working closely with you in getting things moving helps in ensuring the quality and experience.

I can hire lawyers and accountants directly. Why should I go with Wazzeer?

Wazzeer acts as a one-stop solution for all your legal and accounting requirements. We understand your needs and bring in the partners best suited (in terms of expertise, experience, area of work, location and cost effectiveness) for the work. You need not go out every time to get the best person for the job or rely on the same professional for all your requirements. We believe that hiring the professional does not solve the problem. The challenge lies in getting the work done. We ensure that. We work closely with you and the partners (lawyers, CA and CS) to get the work delivered in a professional manner.

How can I contact Wazzeer if I need some urgent help?

Once we start working with you, we will have someone from our team in touch with you. His or her coordinates (phone number, email id) along with that of the partners working with you will be shared. You can contact them any time as per your requirements. In case you have not engaged with us and need us to help you urgently on any of your requirements, please go to our website and fill in the details. We will get in touch with you shortly. In case something is really urgent, please mail us at enquire@wazzeer.com.

If I hire a professional through Wazzeer, do I get better advice from the professional?

We ensure that we partner with professionals who take their professional ethics seriously. No matter how you engage with them, their advice will be the best as per their understanding and professional capabilities. What we at Wazzeer ensure is that your experience is seamless and you do not end up wasting your time in follow-ups. We get the work done for you while you focus on growing your business.
