About Us

Wazzeer is using technology to simplify hiring lawyers and accountants, and the way they deliver their service. We intend to become the platform that enables end clients, entrepreneurs and SMEs to select the best suited professional, get a complete picture of their work and get it done seamlessly allowing them to focus on their core business. With a set of handpicked partners, we are removing the information asymmetry, bringing in professionalism and making the process intuitive, convenient and professional. In other words, the kind of service we want for ourselves.
