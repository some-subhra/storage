What are the types of Business Incorporations that are available in India.

Majorly there are two types of Company Incorporation :

  • Unlimited Liability Companies: No difference between the firm and the founders. Founders' liability is unlimited and the company will not have continuous existence, i.e., on the demise of founders company cease to exist.
  • Limited Liability Companies: Company is a separate legal entity and hence offers Limited liability to founders, i.e., it limits the liability only up to the assets of the company. These types of companies are registered by Registrar of Companies under Indian Companies Act-2013
